You have a beautiful soul, and I am so proud of the person you are becoming. Seeing you handle life with such grace makes me admire you more every single day.

You have a fire inside you that can conquer anything. May your year be filled with big adventures, academic success, and all the happiness you deserve.

Whatever path you choose, remember that I am not just watching from the sidelines—I am walking right beside you. I promise to hold your hand through the challenges and celebrate every single win, no matter how small. Your happiness is my happiness, and seeing you grow is my greatest joy.

Keep shining, Chichay. The world is a much better place because you are in it, and my world is complete because I have you.

With all my heart,
Eboy
